Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 14, 2025

Overview

Tyumi is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our AI assistant platform.

Information We Don't Collect

Tyumi is designed with privacy in mind:

  • No Server Storage: We do not store any of your conversations, images, or personal data on our servers
  • No Analytics: We do not collect usage analytics or tracking data
  • No Personal Information: We do not require or collect personal information like names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • No Account Creation: No user accounts or profiles are created or maintained

Local Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser:

  • Conversations: Chat history is stored in your browser's local storage
  • Images: Generated images are stored locally in your browser
  • API Keys: Your API keys are stored securely in your browser's local storage and never transmitted to our servers
  • Settings: Your preferences and settings are stored locally

Third-Party Services

When you use Tyumi, you may interact with third-party AI services:

  • AI Providers: Your messages are sent directly to your chosen AI provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, etc.) according to their privacy policies
  • API Communications: Communications with AI services are encrypted and go directly from your browser to the service provider
  • Local Models: When using local Ollama models, no data leaves your device

Data Security

  • Encryption: All API communications are encrypted using HTTPS
  • Local Only: Your data remains on your device and is not accessible to us
  • Browser Security: Data security relies on your browser's built-in security features

Data Retention

  • User Control: You have complete control over your data and can delete it at any time
  • Browser Clearing: Data will be removed if you clear your browser's storage
  • No Remote Backup: We do not maintain backups of your data

Cookies and Tracking

Tyumi does not use cookies for tracking or analytics. Any browser storage used is solely for application functionality and storing your local data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated policy available on this page with a new effective date.
